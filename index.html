Photo Lab

Photo Lab is a C program that works as a pseudo-Photoshop program for manipulating an image. Program presents users with a menu to select a different number of options that will be applied to an image. Filters include: black and white filter, edge detection, shuffling, brightness, mirroring, hue, rotating/flip, resizing, and saturation. Utilizes data structure and pointer system, and output image is saved to a website for viewing.

Big Active Data: UCI Study Party Finder

Stress tested a developing extension for AsterixDB known as Big Active Data AsterixDB. BAD is designed to deliver data of interest to a scalable number of users without compromising relationships between data, the enrichment of data, and retrospective Big Data analytics. To test this system, my team created a new mobile application known as the UCI Study Party Finder, which mimics the party finding systems seen in video games. Users can search for a UCI-specific course and find the list of other students in the same course who are likewise looking for studymates. Additional options such as preferred party size, meeting time, location, and purpose of meeting are also available.
